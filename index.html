Our Services

Video Engineering

With over 14 years of experience on corporate events you can rest assured that the video portion of your event is in very good hands. My skills range from avanced multi destination switchers (Barco Encore, E2, S3 and Vista Spyder) to production switchers (Barco, Panasonic and Grass Valley) to multicamera setup and camera calibration and the most current playback and record technologies

Production Management

Need to add some contract PM support to expand your business. I've been production managing for over 10 years and the results speak for themself. Projection
